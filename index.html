Hello There

We Are Avril

Here at Avril, we focus on relaying brand presence in the most efficient ways possible. We are a team of creatives and highly detailed technical experts that create websites and digital campaigns that are customer focussed and results driven.

What We Do

It all ends and begins with the customer. We take the time to understand your business and aspirations, your market and your product or service. We help define what makes your brand great.

Brand Identity

Online traffic means nothing if your customer can't identify your message. Our team is filled with creatives and artists that will help you summarize your brand through a visually attractive and easily comprehendible online presence.

Web Development

As self-proclaimed nerds, we love to build websites that are user friendly, mobile responsive, and on the cutting edge of technology. Our websites are beautiful and relay your brand in the most efficent way possible.

Digital Marketing

Print ads, radio, television? No thanks. Although it may seem edgy, here at Avril we fundamentally oppose traditional advertising means. We know that in 2018, these means of advertising lose money. We focus on tangible marketing strategies such as SEO, social media campaigns, influencer marketing, and more.

Social Media

Not sure how something like Instagram or Facebook is relevent? No worries. Here at Avril, we can set up and guide you through the most popular social media platforms out there. Not interested and want to leave it all up to us? No worries, that works too.

SEO

Here at Avril, we are numbers obsessed. We study algorithms for you, to make sure that your business reaches the top of your local search results. With our help, your product or services will drop right into your ideal customers' laps.

And more

Need a launch party? A video campaign? Something else? We probably have means to do that, or we can use our expert connections to outsource and find the perfect person that can. Talk to us and find out the possibilities.
